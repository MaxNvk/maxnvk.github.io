Crowd Pleaser
Crispy Rice Squares

Enjoy this recipe from our friends at Rice-A-Roni. Stir up a showstopping side with spice-filled rice and veggies.

Topping

Cooking instructions:

Base:

Step 1

In a large pan, saut rice-pasta mix over medium heat until vermicelli is golden brown, stirring frequently.

Step 2

Slowly stir in water and seasonings; bring to a boil.

Step 3

Cover and reduce heat to low. After 10 minutes remove pan cover. Simmer for another 15 minutes uncovered, stirring frequently. Rice-a-Roni mixture should be very thick.

Step 4

Remove from heat. Line square 8 pan with parchment paper. Press cooked rice into pan to form a thin layer.

Step 5

Top with another layer of parchment paper and cover pan with another 8 square pan for pressing. Garnish with a drizzle of crema, a squeeze of lime and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes as optional toppings.

Step 6

Chill in freezer for 1 - 2 hours or until rice forms a solid slab.

Step 7

Remove from freezer and remove rice slab from pan.

Step 8

Using a knife, cut into 2-inch squares.

Step 9

Add 1/4 cup oil to a pan on medium low heat, add the rice squares once oil is hot and cook for about 3- 4 minutes on each side or until crispy and browned.

Step 10

Remove and top with your choice of desired toppings.

Step 11

Air Fryer Option: Once rice squares are cut, place them in the Air Fryer at 400F for 5 minutes or until crisp and golden.

Topping:

Step 1

To make avocado salsa dice the avocado, tomato, and onion. Combine in a bowl.

Step 2

Finely chop jalapeo and cilantro. Add to bowl.

Step 3

Toss together with lime juice.

Step 4

Place about 1 tbsp of salsa on crispy rice square.

Step 5

Garnish with a drizzle of crema and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.

Nutritional Information
